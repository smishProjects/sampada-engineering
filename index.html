About Us

Our Company

SHUBHAM ENGINEERING WORKS is a Proprietorship company, Formed in 1997 is Primarily in Manufacturing of All Kinds of Extrusion Profile Dies. The Company Has made a steady progress in the field of Extrusion Dies. (i.e. in Door Panel Dies). We Specialize in Door Panel Dies, Multi panel As well as Single panel Dies. Up to 30 Other Products include Selling panel, Cassing N Capping, R-P.V.C, U-P.V.C & S-P.V.C

Our Culture

The company enjoy a good reputation among the Major extruder dies making Company in Mumbai. The company is having well Equipped Tool room. We have Devoted & Motivated Skilled Workforce to take on new challenges And continually focus on to making better quality dies, Through continuous Development. We assure Quick Development, Fast Delivery With Best Quality. And Also very competitive Rates. Factory trained Qualified service Engineers always in top gear to support our costumer at any location in India.
