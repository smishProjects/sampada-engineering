About Company

A Legacy of Three Decades – Crafting Reliable Solutions, Building a Better Tomorrow

Established in April 1995 by Mr. Sandeep Dattatray Lokhande, Sampada Engineering Works has been a trusted leader in precision manufacturing for nearly three decades. Based in Virar East, Palghar District, we cater to the automobile and electrical industries, offering high-quality sheet metal components and innovative solutions.
